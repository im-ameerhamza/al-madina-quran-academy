Madani Noorani Qaida

500 Enrolled
04 Months Duration

Course Details

Days: As Per Your Choice Class Time: 30 Minutes Total Weekly Hours: 03 hours Language: Urdu / English

“Noorani Qaida is a beginner’s book used to teach basic Quranic reading. It was developed by Moulvi Noor Muhammad Ludhyanvi in the Indian subcontinent and is widely used in Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, and other countries where people learn to read the Quran in Arabic.”

Here’s what it covers and why it’s important:

Purpose
  • It helps children and new learners read the Quran correctly by teaching Arabic letters, their pronunciations, and how to join them into words.
  • It focuses on tajweed rules (proper pronunciation) from the very start.
Key Contents
  1. Arabic Alphabet – Recognizing each letter in its isolated form.
  2. Letter Sounds – Learning the correct sounds (makharij) for each letter.
  3. Vowel Signs (Harakaat) – Fatha, Kasra, Dammah, Sukoon, etc.
  4. Tanween & Madd – Nasal sounds and elongation.
  5. Letter Joining – How to connect letters to form words.
  6. Basic Tajweed – Rules like ghunna (nasal sound), qalqalah (echoing sound), and stopping signs.
Importance
  • It’s a step-by-step method so learners can move from recognizing letters to fluent Quranic recitation.
  • Used in madrasas, mosques, and online classes around the world.
  • It builds a strong foundation for later learning of tajweed and hifz (memorization).
